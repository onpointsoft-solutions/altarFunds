What We Do

OnPoint Pay provides a comprehensive payment ecosystem designed specifically for African businesses and their customers.

Process payments from M-Pesa, cards, and bank transfers instantly. Our system handles multiple payment methods with automatic currency conversion and real-time confirmation.
